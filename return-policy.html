Return & Refund Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. Return Eligibility

We accept returns for the following reasons:

  • Product is damaged or defective upon delivery
  • Wrong item delivered (different from what you ordered)
  • Expired or near-expiry products delivered
  • Missing items from your order

Returns must be requested within 24 hours of delivery.

2. Non-Returnable Items

Due to health and safety reasons, we cannot accept returns for:

  • Opened food items or beverages
  • Used personal care products
  • Products with tampered packaging
  • Items without original packaging

3. Return Process

1

Contact Us Immediately

Call us at +91 94168 91710 within 24 hours of delivery.

2

Provide Details

Share your order ID, issue description, and photos of the damaged/incorrect item.

3

Arrange Pickup

Our team will arrange pickup of the item at no additional cost to you.

4

Get Replacement/Refund

We'll deliver the correct item or process your refund within 3-5 business days.

4. Refund Process

Refunds will be processed as follows:

  • Cash on Delivery Orders: Cash refund or replacement product
  • Refunds typically processed within 3-5 business days
  • You'll receive confirmation via email and WhatsApp once refund is processed
