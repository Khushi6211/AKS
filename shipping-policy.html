Shipping & Delivery Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. Delivery Areas

We currently deliver to the following areas:

  • Barara, Ambala, Haryana
  • Surrounding localities within a 10 km radius of our store

If you're unsure whether we deliver to your area, please contact us at +91 94168 91710 before placing your order.

2. Delivery Charges

Orders below ₹500: ₹40
Orders ₹500 and above: FREE

Our delivery charges are transparent and will be clearly displayed in your cart before checkout.

3. Delivery Time

We strive to deliver your orders as quickly as possible:

  • Standard Delivery: 24-48 hours from order placement
  • Same-Day Delivery: Available for orders placed before 12:00 PM (subject to availability)
  • Express Delivery: Contact us for urgent requirements

Note: Delivery times may vary during peak seasons, festivals, or adverse weather conditions. We'll keep you informed of any delays.

5. Tracking Your Order

Stay updated on your order status:

  • Email Notifications: Receive updates at every stage of your order
  • WhatsApp Updates: Get real-time status updates on WhatsApp
  • Order History: Login to your account and visit "Order History" to track your order
  • Call Us: Contact us at +91 94168 91710 for immediate assistance

6. Delivery Issues

If you face any issues with your delivery, please contact us immediately:

Common Issues & Solutions:

  • Damaged Items: Take photos and contact us within 24 hours for replacement
  • Incorrect Items: We'll arrange pickup and deliver the correct item at no extra cost
  • Missing Items: Report within 24 hours for immediate resolution
  • Delivery Delays: We'll keep you informed and prioritize your order

7. Undeliverable Orders

Orders may be undeliverable if:

  • The delivery address is incorrect or incomplete
  • No one is available to receive the order after multiple delivery attempts
  • Customer is unreachable via phone or WhatsApp
  • Delivery location is inaccessible or outside our delivery zone

In such cases, we'll contact you to arrange redelivery or order cancellation as per your preference.
