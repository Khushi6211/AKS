Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

General Questions

What is Arun Karyana Store?

Arun Karyana Store is your trusted neighborhood grocery store in Barara, Ambala, serving the community since 1977. We offer a wide range of quality household essentials, food items, beverages, soaps, detergents, and personal care products at competitive prices.

Where are you located?

We are located at Railway Road, Barara, Ambala, Haryana 133201. Visit us in person to experience our warm hospitality and browse our extensive product range.

What are your opening hours?

We are open 7 days a week:
• Monday - Friday: 8:00 AM - 9:00 PM
• Saturday: 8:00 AM - 10:00 PM
• Sunday: 9:00 AM - 8:00 PM

Ordering & Delivery

How do I place an order?

You can place an order through our website by browsing our products, adding items to your cart, and proceeding to checkout. Fill in your delivery details, and we'll take care of the rest!

Is there a minimum order value?

There is no minimum order value. However, orders above ₹500 qualify for free delivery.

What are the delivery charges?

Delivery charges are ₹40 for orders below ₹500. Orders of ₹500 and above enjoy free delivery!

How long does delivery take?

We strive to deliver orders within 24-48 hours of placement, depending on your location in Barara and surrounding areas. You'll receive updates on your order status via email and WhatsApp.

Can I track my order?

Yes! Once you place an order, you can track its status by logging into your account and visiting the "Order History" page. You'll also receive email and WhatsApp notifications when your order status changes.

Payment & Pricing

What payment methods do you accept?

Currently, we accept Cash on Delivery (COD). Digital payment options will be available soon.

Do you offer any discounts or promotions?

Yes! We regularly run special offers and promotions. Check our "Offers" section on the homepage for current deals. We also have promo codes that you can apply during checkout for additional discounts.

Are prices on the website the same as in-store?

Yes, we maintain consistent pricing across both our online store and physical location to ensure fairness and transparency for all customers.

Account & Registration

Do I need to create an account to place an order?

No, you can place orders as a guest. However, creating an account allows you to track orders, save addresses, view order history, and enjoy faster checkout in the future.

How do I create an account?

Click on "Login" in the top right corner, then select "Sign Up". Fill in your details (name, email, phone, and password) and you're all set!

I forgot my password. What should I do?

On the login page, click "Forgot Password?". Enter your registered email address, and we'll send you a link to reset your password.

Returns & Cancellations

Can I cancel my order?

What is your return policy?

We accept returns for damaged or incorrect items within 24 hours of delivery. Please refer to our Return Policy page for detailed information.
