Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use our services:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, delivery address
  • Order Information: Purchase history, payment preferences, delivery details
  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, device type
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, products viewed, time spent on site

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Process and deliver your orders
  • Send order confirmations and status updates
  • Provide customer support and respond to inquiries
  • Improve our products and services
  • Send promotional offers (with your consent)
  • Prevent fraud and ensure security

3. Information Sharing

We do NOT sell your personal information. We may share data with:

  • Service Providers: Delivery partners, payment processors (for future services)

  • Business Transfers: In case of merger, acquisition, or asset sale

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Object to data processing

Contact us at contact@arunkaryanastore.com to exercise these rights.

6. Cookies

We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience, remember your preferences, and analyze site traffic. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

7. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
